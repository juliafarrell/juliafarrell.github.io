  • Know Your Normal With Norma
    Norma is a mobile app and IoT device that encourages the habit of monthly breast self exams, one of the most effective ways to detect breast abnormalities.
    Help us help you

How it works

Norma is currently in its Beta stage as a breast tracking journal. Simply click and drag any abnormalities you find with the visual in-app journal to monitor your breast health. We encourage women to consult with their doctor if they notice changes they find alarming, or notice pain in their breasts. With Norma, you know your normal so you can detect what's not.

Please Take Our Survey

The survey below will only take you about two minutes to fill out, and it will help us change the world. It asks about how you currently handle your breast health, what your family history with breast cancer is like, and some other basic information that helps us build the app you need and deserve. All responses are confidential and instrumental to our success. Every response helps us create the world we want to live in!
