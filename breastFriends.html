Norma's Network of Breast Friends

Introducing The Norma Network of Breast Friends

Wow, it’s only been six weeks since we conceptualized Norma, but the response from women around the world has been outstanding! The six woman team at Norma is working hard to bring our app and IoT device to life and your support is simply invaluable.

Knowing your normal is one of the most effective ways to detect something that’s not. At Norma, we are dedicated to providing you the tools and resources to take charge of your breast health so you ‘know your normal’. Our goal is to launch Norma in October 2018, which is Breast Cancer Awareness month, but we don’t want to wait until October to start helping women change their breast health habits.

Let’s Empower Women Together!

That’s why we have decided to launch the Norma Network of Breast Friends on Apr. 1! This grassroots campaign will be spearheaded by you, our Breast Friend brand advocates, and encourages the women you care about to #feelitonthefirst.

So how does our campaign work? On the first of each month, the Norma team will send our Breast Friends a link to a new breast health blog that they can share with their family and friends. We just ask you text this link and a reminder to #feelinonthefirst with the women you care most about. With this personal reminder, our hope is to get more women engaged with their breast health so that they know their normal and can detect what’s not.

Join our Breast Friends!

We already have 11 women signed up and would love to have you join our Breast Friend Network. In return, we can feature you on our blog and social media channels. As we build our app and IoT device, you will be one of the first to access our products and will be able to share your feedback with us before we go live.

Ready to join our game-changing network of powerful women who are taking charge of their breast health? Just email us at founders@knowyournorma.com and we’ll get you set up!

Thanks so much for your support as we all tackle the many breast health challenges women around the world face.
